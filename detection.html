Crewneck

Crewnecks are typically made from soft and comfortable fabrics such as cotton, wool, or a blend of materials. They are known for their relaxed fit, providing a comfortable and casual look. The sleeves of a crewneck can vary in length, ranging from full-length sleeves to three-quarter or short sleeves, depending on the design.

 

One of the key features of a crewneck is its versatility. It can be easily paired with various bottoms, such as jeans, trousers, or skirts, making it suitable for both casual and semi-formal occasions. It can be worn alone or layered with other clothing items, allowing for different styling options.
